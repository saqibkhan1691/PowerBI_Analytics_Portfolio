# 🕊️ NayePankh Social Impact Analytics

> **Empowering Social Change Through Data-Driven Insights**

## 📌 Overview

**NayePankh Social Impact Analytics** is a Power BI-based data analytics project developed to support evidence-based decision-making for social welfare initiatives. The project analyzes child nutrition and women empowerment indicators across Indian states using publicly available government datasets.

The goal of this project is to identify high-priority regions and provide insights that can help organizations like **NayePankh Foundation** plan targeted interventions and maximize social impact.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify states with high levels of child malnutrition.
* Analyze women empowerment and health indicators.
* Compare social indicators across states.
* Generate actionable insights for social welfare programs.
* Support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📊 Datasets Used

### 1. National Family Health Survey (NFHS-5), 2019-2021

Contains information related to:

* Female literacy
* Anaemia among women
* Health indicators
* Child health
* Nutrition indicators

### 2. Poshan Tracker Dataset (2022–2024)

Contains state-wise malnutrition indicators:

* Stunting
* Wasting
* Underweight prevalence

**Source:** Government of India (data.gov.in)
